Our Story

Waterly was born from a vision to revolutionize water supply management through cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. Founded as a comprehensive government initiative, we've been at the forefront of ensuring clean, reliable water access for communities across the nation.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to bridge the gap between traditional water distribution systems and modern digital solutions. Today, we serve over 500,000 homes across 50+ cities, maintaining an unprecedented 99.9% uptime through innovative infrastructure and smart monitoring systems.
